## Course Materials Organization

All course materials are organized in the `coursework/Spring2026/` directory:

- **Cryptography**: `/coursework/Spring2026/cryptography/`
- **Software Verification**: `/coursework/Spring2026/software-verification/`

Each course has:
- `syllabus.yaml` - Course schedule and lecture metadata
- `AGENT_INSTRUCTIONS.md` - Instructions for course assistant agents
- `_lectures/` - Organized lecture materials (slides, transcripts, videos, notes)
- `_assignments/` - Assignment materials
- `_resources/` - Additional resources and references
- `_output/` - Generated content (markdown, LaTeX)

## Notes

- Course materials are automatically organized by dedicated agents

- Calendars are synchronized between Canvas and Gmail
- Daily summaries are created after each lecture
